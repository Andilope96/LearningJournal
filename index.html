Welcome to my Learning Journal — I’m Andreas.

I work as an IT consultant and build apps and automations with Microsoft Power Platform. In parallel, I’m systematically developing full-stack skills. This journal is my public learning log: what I’m studying, what I’m building, and the lessons I’m collecting as I go.

My goal is to become capable of building complete, reliable applications — structured, maintainable, and production-ready. And to help others with my journey to become a fullstack developer through my journal.

If you want to see my work, you can find my projects and code on GitHub. For networking, feedback, or collaboration, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn.
